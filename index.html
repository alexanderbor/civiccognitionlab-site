Civic Cognition Lab

Civic Cognition Lab is a research group at the intersection of politics and psychology, studying how human cognition and moral intuitions shape democratic norms, institutions, and behavior.

Led by Alexander Bor, the lab hosts the ERC Starting Grant Intuitive Foundations of Democratic Norms.
